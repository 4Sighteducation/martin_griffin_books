## üìã IMMEDIATE ACTIONS NEEDED (Do These First)

### Step 1: Submit to Google Search Console (10 minutes)

1. **Go to** [Google Search Console](https://search.google.com/search-console)
2. **Click** "Add Property"
3. **Enter** your domain: `martingriffinbooks.com`
4. **Verify ownership** via one of these methods:
   - **DNS record** (recommended if you control domain)
   - **HTML file upload**
   - **Meta tag** (add to site header)

5. **Submit your sitemap:**
   - URL: `https://www.martingriffinbooks.com/sitemap-index.xml`
   - Wait for Google to crawl (1-2 days)

**Why this matters:** This is THE most important SEO step. Without it, Google might not even know your site exists.

---

### Step 2: Verify with Bing Webmaster Tools (5 minutes)

1. **Go to** [Bing Webmaster Tools](https://www.bing.com/webmasters)
2. **Add your site**
3. **Import from Google Search Console** (easiest option)
4. **Submit sitemap**

**Why this matters:** Bing powers ~30% of searches (including Yahoo, DuckDuckGo). Often overlooked but valuable traffic.
